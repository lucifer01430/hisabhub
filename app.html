User Guide
Purpose of the App

HisabHub is an offline-first personal finance web application designed to help you manage daily income and expenses in a simple, private, and organized way. It allows you to record transactions, monitor monthly spending, and understand your financial habits through visual insights — all without requiring an internet connection or external account system.

The app focuses on practical day-to-day budgeting, giving you a clear snapshot of your income, expenses, savings, and remaining balance so you can make smarter financial decisions with minimal effort.

How to Use
  1. Create your account by registering with your name, username, password, and recovery PIN for local password reset.
  2. Add your monthly income such as salary or other earnings to establish your financial baseline.
  3. Log expenses daily by entering the date, category, amount, and optional notes for each transaction.
  4. Track activity visually using Calendar View for date-wise spending or List View for detailed records.
  5. Analyze spending patterns in the Insights tab to see category distribution, totals, and financial trends.
  6. Generate monthly PDF reports anytime to keep a professional summary of your finances for records or review.
Key Features
  • Monthly income tracking with automatic balance calculation.
  • Category-based expense logging for structured records.
  • Calendar, list, and insight views for multiple tracking styles.
  • One-click professional PDF export for monthly summaries.
  • Secure local storage ensuring your data stays on your device.
Why This App is Different
  • Offline-first architecture — works fully without internet or backend.
  • Privacy-focused design — no personal or financial data is sent to servers.
  • Lightweight and fast — loads instantly and runs smoothly on mobile or desktop.
  • Clean dashboard experience — focuses on clarity instead of complex finance tools.
  • Built for real-life budgeting — simple enough for daily use yet powerful for monthly review.
