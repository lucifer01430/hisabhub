About the App

HisabHub is a privacy-focused personal finance web application designed to help you record, organize, and understand your daily income and expenses with ease. Whether you are managing your monthly salary, tracking small day-to-day spending, or reviewing long-term financial habits, the app provides a simple and distraction-free environment to stay in control of your money.

Built with an offline-first architecture, all your financial data is stored securely on your own device using browser local storage. This means you can use the app anytime without internet access, and your information remains private — no external servers, accounts, or data sharing required.

The dashboard gives you a clear overview of your monthly income, total expenses, remaining balance, and savings in real time. Visual insights and categorized summaries help you quickly identify where your money goes, recognize spending patterns, and make smarter budgeting decisions.

With one-click PDF report generation, you can export professional monthly summaries for personal records, budgeting reviews, or sharing when needed. HisabHub is built to be lightweight, mobile-friendly, and practical — focusing on simplicity, speed, and privacy to make everyday financial tracking effortless.
